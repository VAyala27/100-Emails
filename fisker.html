Clean Water

The “Clean Water” configuration features the Blue Planet exterior color and reminds Henrik and our entire team that we’re here to drive sustainability, design, and innovation. The color designers took inspiration from the brilliant blues we see on our oceans when viewing photographs of Earth from space. When paired with the Maliblu interior and F6 Vortex wheels, this configuration creates an eco-friendly vehicle with sleek and imposing curb appeal that is unmistakably Fisker.

Elegant Sky

“Elegant Sky” is anchored by Silver Lining, a bright, metallic silver that’s strikingly bold and always optimistic, especially when paired with the aerodynamic 22” F3a SlipStream wheels and a Sea Salt interior that features whites that shine bright like the California sunshine. Looking to the clouds for inspiration, “Elegant Sky” reminds us that it takes a team of dreamers, idealists, and optimists to build a car and a company focused on creating A Clean Future for All.

Midnight Rebel

The “Midnight Rebel” configuration pairs Night Drive exterior color and 22” F5b Black AirGlider wheels to showcase an inky-onyx black that's silky, glossy, and satisfyingly dark. “Midnight Rebel” is complimented by the rich, balanced, blue-on-black hues and luxurious, soft-touch materials of Maliblu, making it the perfect vehicle for a late-night getaway.
